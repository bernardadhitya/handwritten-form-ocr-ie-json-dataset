 

SECRECY AGREEMENT

representatives of G.D Societa per Azioni ("G.D"), hereby
agree not to disclose any information received from R. J.
Reynolds Tobacco Company ("RJR") with respect to RJR's
development of a new product (hereinafter referred to a6
"Project CAL") or the existence or nature of RJR's
discussions with G.D concerning new package design concepts
for Project CAL, to any third party or to any other employee
of G.D without the prior written consent of RJR
